Description

This metadata application profile is intended for use by researchers in the geosciences to track their geologic samples. The items being described are rock samples collected during fieldwork. While there are well-developed systems to track publications and datasets from research projects, metadata for physical samples is still an area where much work is needed.

This metadata application profile will show a way for departments with geoscience researchers to keep track of the samples collected during research projects within the department. The schema has elements for both the person who collected the sample and the PI of the project, if those two are different. This will allow departments to track the samples collected by each person involved in fieldwork, as well as the overall samples collected within each PI's lab.
